About me

I’m currently finishing the first year of my Master’s degree in Data Science and Advanced Analytics. My background is in Corporate Finance, which is quite a different field. I’ll admit, I struggled a bit at the start, but to my great pleasure, I find myself more excited about turning raw data into actionable insights with every project I take on.

As a data scientist, I feel like we take on the roles of a detective, a wizard, and a storyteller at different stages of a project. Beyond that, we need to be highly versatile, since interdisciplinary work is part of the nature of this field. I consider myself an easily adaptable and curious person, which makes this dynamic especially fulfilling for me. My strong foundation in finance also allows me to approach problems from a different perspective, an interesting bridge between data insights and business domain.

I primarily use Python, but I also have experience with SQL and a bit of R. Feel free to explore some of my projects in the section below! .
