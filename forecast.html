Ana Pedro Caleiro Portfolio

To remain competitive in the smart power distribution market, businesses must be able to identify sales trends and anticipate future demand with precision. Siemens understands this well. They aimed to move beyond traditional manual forecasting methods by adopting a more intelligent, data-driven approach.

A team of specialists inside the company developed a sales forecasting solution and later gave us the opportunity to independently explore and develop our own solution using their real data. This project followed the CRISP-DM methodology and employed machine learning models such as XGBoost and ARIMA to predict monthly sales across different product groups, covering the period from May 2022 to February 2023. Historical sales data from October 2018 to April 2022, along with macroeconomic indicators, were used to enhance the modelâ€™s accuracy and adaptability.

By forecasting future sales across product groups, the company can better align production planning, inventory management, and strategic resource allocation.
