Ana Pedro Caleiro Portfolio

As part of this project, I worked on developing a data-driven customer segmentation strategy for ABCDEats Inc., a fictional food delivery company. The goal was to analyze customer data collected over three months across three cities to uncover distinct customer segments based on behaviors, preferences, and economic value.

To achieve this, I implemented unsupervised machine learning techniques, including K-means, Hierarchical Clustering, Self-Organizing Maps (SOMs), DBSCAN, Mean-Shift, and Gaussian Mixture models. The project involved a full data pipeline starting from data cleaning and preprocessing to feature selection, clustering, and evaluation. By integrating multiple segmentation perspectives, we provided ABCDEats with actionable insights to enhance customer engagement, optimize marketing efforts, and improve retention. Additionally, I leveraged data visualization techniques with streamlit to profile over 30,000 records, making the findings accessible and actionable for stakeholders.

Python (eg. Scikit-Learn, Numpy, Pandas, Matplotib, Seaborn, Streamlit, Plotly) | Grade: 20
