Ana Pedro Caleiro Portfolio

Recheio, a long-standing supermarket chain serving professional customers, recognized the need to modernize its customer engagement strategy through data-driven recommendation systems. They gave us the opportunity to explore real company data to enhance their existing solution.

To this end, our project aimed to build a dual-solution: a customer segmentation model and a recommendation system personalised to Recheioâ€™s needs. First, we explored clustering techniques like K-Means, DBSCAN, and Self-Organizing Maps to segment clients into six distinct behavioral groups. These insights formed the foundation for tailored recommendation strategies. We then implemented multiple recommendation methods, including Collaborative Filtering, Apriori-based Association Rules, and custom logic for Smart Basket and Substitute Product suggestions.

By aligning personalized recommendations with strategic customer clusters, Recheio will be able to improve customer satisfaction and drive sales growth.
